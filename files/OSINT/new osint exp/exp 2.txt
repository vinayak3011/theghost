Part 2: Analyze the Header

Open a new browser tab.

 https://mxtoolbox.com/EmailHeaders.aspx.

Paste your copied header into the big text box.

Click the "Analyze Header" button.



Part 3: Find the Delay (Using Your Table)

The tool will show you a report. Scroll down until you see the "Routing Path" table. 

Look at the column named Delay.

Find the row that has the biggest number (the longest time).

("0 seconds", which means the email was super fast. In a different email, you might see "10 minutes" or "300 seconds".)

In that same row with the biggest delay, look at the server name in the From column.

That From server is the one that caused the delay. It held the email for a long time before sending it to the By server.

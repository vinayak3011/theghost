Part 1: Find Emails with theHarvester

Open Kali Linux and open the Terminal.

To complete the experiment, type the main command and press Enter:

	theHarvester -d infosec.edu -b google

Wait for the tool to finish. It will show you a list of any emails (like name@infosec.edu) and subdomains it found.

If your examiner asks for more examples, you are ready. Show them these other commands from your notes to prove you know the tool well:

To search a different domain (like your college):
	theHarvester -d sakec.ac.in -b yahoo

To search for employee names on LinkedIn:
	theHarvester -d nist.gov -b linkedin

To search with a limit (so it's faster):
	theHarvester -d nist.gov -b google -l 100 -v
